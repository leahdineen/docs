## Overview

The `content_quality` detector provides a comprehensive assessment of overall video content quality, analyzing multiple factors including visual appeal, production value, engagement potential, and overall professionalism using AI vision models.

**No configuration required** - This detector works with default settings.

## Configuration

This detector does not require any configuration parameters. Include it with an empty config object:

```json
{
  "detector_name": "content_quality",
  "config": {}
}
```

## Example Configuration

```json
{
  "detector_name": "content_quality",
  "config": {}
}
```

## Quality Assessment Categories

### Production Value
- **Professional Setup** - Studio-quality lighting, equipment, and environment
- **Technical Execution** - Camera work, editing, and post-production quality
- **Content Planning** - Evidence of preparation and intentional content creation
- **Brand Presentation** - Professional representation and messaging

### Visual Appeal
- **Aesthetic Quality** - Overall visual attractiveness and style
- **Color Grading** - Consistent and appealing color treatment
- **Composition** - Rule of thirds, framing, and visual balance
- **Visual Variety** - Multiple angles, shots, and visual interest

### Content Engagement
- **Pacing** - Appropriate rhythm and flow of content
- **Energy Level** - Creator enthusiasm and engagement
- **Authenticity** - Genuine personality and natural presentation
- **Storytelling** - Narrative structure and content organization

### Technical Quality
- **Resolution** - Video clarity and sharpness
- **Audio Quality** - Clear, balanced audio without issues
- **Stability** - Steady camera work without excessive shake
- **Focus** - Proper focus maintenance throughout content

## Quality Scoring

### Excellent (0.9-1.0)
- Professional-grade production
- Exceptional visual and audio quality
- High engagement potential
- Minimal technical issues

### Very Good (0.8-0.89)
- High production standards
- Strong visual appeal
- Good engagement factors
- Minor technical considerations

### Good (0.7-0.79)
- Solid content quality
- Acceptable production value
- Average engagement potential
- Some improvement areas

### Fair (0.6-0.69)
- Basic quality standards
- Limited production value
- Below-average engagement
- Multiple improvement needs

### Poor (0.0-0.59)
- Substandard quality
- Poor production value
- Low engagement potential
- Significant technical issues

## Improvement Recommendations

### High Priority
- Major technical issues affecting viewing experience
- Critical audio or visual problems
- Fundamental production shortcomings

### Medium Priority
- Moderate quality improvements
- Enhanced production techniques
- Better equipment or setup recommendations

### Low Priority
- Minor optimizations
- Style and aesthetic enhancements
- Advanced production techniques

## Quality Factors Analysis

- **Lighting Assessment** - Natural vs. artificial lighting quality
- **Audio Analysis** - Clarity, levels, and background noise
- **Visual Stability** - Camera shake and movement quality
- **Focus Management** - Sharpness and depth of field usage
- **Color Science** - Accurate colors and white balance

## Use Cases

- **Content Optimization** - Identifying areas for quality improvement
- **Brand Standards** - Ensuring content meets quality requirements
- **Creator Development** - Providing feedback for skill improvement
- **Campaign Quality** - Maintaining consistent production standards
